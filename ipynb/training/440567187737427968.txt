A group of Vietnam veterans sued the U.S. government Monday, alleging they were given other-than-honorable military discharges for violations that would today be attributed to the psychological stress of combat.. The plaintiffs said the military has "systematically" refused to upgrade their discharges despite evidence they were suffering from post-traumatic stress disorder connected to wartime service.
