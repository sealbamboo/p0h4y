SYDNEY—TPG Inc. and Carlyle Group launched an initial public offering of Healthscope Ltd., which could be Australia's largest listing in four years, in a further sign that activity among global buyout firms is intensifying down under.. The IPO of Healthscope, which will value the hospital operator at up to 3.81 billion Australian dollars (US$3.59 billion), aims to feed investor demand for health-care stocks as Australians spend more on everything from doctor's visits to over-the-counter drugs.
