An ambulance has overturned while responding to a 999 call. . It was racing to assist an unconscious patient at a nursing home and was using blue lights and sirens at the time, a police spokesman said.. Drivers were warned to avoid the A449 Penn Road in Wolverhampton following the accident, at the Muchall Road junction, at about 10:30 GMT.. The ambulance crew and a student paramedic were shaken but did not require hospital treatment.. A West Midlands Ambulance Service spokesman said a further ambulance was immediately sent to the nursing home patient.. reported that congestion caused by the partial blockage of the road had been cleared.
