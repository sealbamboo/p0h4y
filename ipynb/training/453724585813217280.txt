Research released Tuesday shows 9.3 million Americans gained insurance coverage since the Affordable Care Act took full effect, though most who bought policies through newly established exchanges weren't uninsured and employers accounted for much of the rise in the newly covered.. The figures from the nonpartisan research firm Rand Corp. are the latest findings to paint an emerging picture of the law's impact.
