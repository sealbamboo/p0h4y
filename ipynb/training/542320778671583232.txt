As health officials push to reduce caesarean births rates have been particularly hard to rein in among one group of patientswomen getting repeat caesareans  The vast majority of women who have caesareans end up repeating the surgical procedure even if they are eligible for a VBAC or vaginal birth after a caesarean A big reason is that many hospitals have stopped offering VBACs because of safety and liability concerns and a shortage of staff and resources to handle emergencies some experts say
