The Carnival Triumph, once nicknamed the "poop cruise," is again plying the seas.. But whether Carnival Corp. can recover from the public-relations debacle remains to be seen. An engine-room fire aboard the Triumph in February left more than 3,000 passengers adrift in the Gulf of Mexico without power or functioning lavatories. The incident drew blanket cable-news coverage, with passengers complaining about overflowing toilets and nasty smells.
