 CVS Caremark Corp has halted sales of certain cold medicines in West Virginia as part of an effort to fight methamphetamine abuse in the state where meth labs have proliferated  The company will no longer sell singleingredient pseudoephedrine often sold under the Sudafed brand name The step follows similar ones by Rite Aid Corp and Walgreen Co in the state said a spokesman for Democratic Sen Joe Manchin who has lobbied companies to stop selling the products
