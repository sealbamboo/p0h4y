File this under enough already FDA officials have asked a compounding pharmacy with which they have repeatedly sparred to recall all of its sterile products over concerns that medicines that are currently circulating may be contaminated and present a risk of illness or injury according to a letter sent yesterday to Downing Labs which operates NuVision Pharmacy in Dallas Texas The move comes after the FDA found a host of problems during an inspection earlier this summer and warned health care providers and consumers not to use the medicines made by the compounder In fact this was the third such warning in  months that the agency issued about NuVision and the safety of its compounded drugs
