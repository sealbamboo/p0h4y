The 39-year-old former Navy pilot told the Silver State Health Insurance Exchange board that he would be leaving March 14, the board confirmed.. The announcement came a week after a public meeting in which Mr. Hager and board members discussed what he described as “website problems, long wait times at the call center, frustrated partners, frustrated consumers and low enrollment.” Some board members were critical of the contractor building the exchange and a decision to revise downwards the number of people expected to enroll this year.
