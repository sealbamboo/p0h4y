In the past I halfheartedly kept a paperandpen food journal but it was timeconsuming and inconvenient to calculate all of those calories so hundreds of them often went unaccounted for each day So when I friend told me about a free online weightloss journal that tracks calories exercise goals and progress I knew it would make food journaling simpler 
                   
                  Filling out my serving estimates online helped me realize that I underestimated portion sizes Even though I was eating mostly nutritious foods I was serving myself too much so I began using measuring cups at home to learn how to identify a healthy serving
