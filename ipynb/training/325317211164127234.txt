Rice is often enjoyed by the bowlful but this week Martha Rose Shulman offers four new ways to make a meal out of smaller portions of brown rice Ms Shulman suggests rice blinis a rice gratin a rice frittata and chard leaves stuffed with herbs and rice And for those who prefer to eat rice in a bowl a simple stirfry is also on the menu Here are five new recipes with rice 
  These are chunkier than pancakes because of the rice but they are more cake than fritter   The chard stems are not wasted adding texture to the flavorful filling for these rolls  The nutty rice makes this seasonal frittata especially substantial  This recipe showcases some unusual greens but plain kale makes a fine substitute  This Mediterraneanstyle dish is delicious hot or cold
