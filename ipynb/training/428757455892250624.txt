 WellPoint Inc. said the people enrolling in new health-law plans are skewing older than its previous individual consumers, but they appear to match the less-healthy pool the company projected when it set its prices.. The insurer's experience is being closely watched because of its big position in the new government marketplaces. The Indianapolis-based company has been the biggest provider of individual plans and is selling them through the government marketplaces in 14 states, including New York and California.
