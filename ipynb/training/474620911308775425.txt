 Sylvia Mathews Burwell is set to take over the tough job of steering implementation of the Affordable Care Act, armed with a wealth of experience in management rather than high-profile political roles.. Thursday's Senate confirmation of Ms. Burwell as secretary of the Department of Health and Human Services, in a 78-17 vote, marks a shift for an agency that has been headed by former state governors for the past 14 years and that over the past several months has been beset by the disastrous Obamacare rollout.
