Celia Wade-Brown, mayor of New Zealand's capital city of Wellington, doesn't think twice about hopping on her bike to go meet visiting dignitaries. Her bike is her main transportation. . In a skirt suit with bike shorts underneath, Ms. Wade-Brown was part of the entourage that greeted Secretary of State Hillary Clinton on her visit in 2010. She said Ms. Clinton and her colleagues would never have known she biked there if it hadn't been mentioned in media reports.
