Mohammad Najih is a typical diabetic in the Middle East: diagnosed at 24, he spent six years ignoring the disease until he was forced to begin injecting insulin. Now aged 32, the Emirati from the desert state of Dubai has vowed to help other Arabs avoid the same mistake.. Last year, the Type 2 diabetic set up an anonymous Twitter account where diabetics can ask questions about the disease and talk to other sufferers.
