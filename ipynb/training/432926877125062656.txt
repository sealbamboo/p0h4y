Same-sex spouses will have the same legal rights in federal matters as other married couples when it comes to issues of bankruptcy, prison visits and testimony, under new policies to be issued Monday by the Justice Department.. The move follows a Supreme Court decision last year that invalidated a key part of the Defense of Marriage Act that defined marriage as solely between a man and a woman.
