PVLMSSA bug had spread between players giving rise to boils abscesses and carbuncles  And in serious cases the infection can lead to lifethreatening illness and cause skin and tissues to die  Doctors have urged players to indulge in less sharing and better hygiene  The condition is caused by a strain of bacteria known as methicillinsensitive Staphylococcus aureus that produces a toxin called PantonValentine leukocidin  Public Health England investigators examined a cluster of suspected infections at the same rugby team between August  and February   They found four cases of the infection
