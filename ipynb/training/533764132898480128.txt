A surgeon critically ill with Ebola arrived in Omaha, Neb., on Saturday afternoon for treatment at a special infectious diseases unit, becoming the 10th patient with the disease to be cared for in the U.S. . A special medical evacuation plane ferrying Martin Salia from Freetown, Sierra Leone, arrived at approximately 2:45 p.m. Central Standard Time at Omaha’s main airport.
