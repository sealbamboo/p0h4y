Calorie counts on a menu are a good start But a  study found that diners are even more motivated to order healthy when those numbers are translated into miles of physical activity Every  calories is roughly equivalent to jogging one mile The next time youre waffling between a calorie sandwich and an calorie pizza slice try thinking about the two extra miles it would take to run off the slice If youre still craving the mozzarella and doughy goodness its worth the calorie splurge
