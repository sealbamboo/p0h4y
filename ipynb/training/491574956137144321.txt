A large, multicountry study examining the genetics of schizophrenia found 108 genetic clusters associated with the disease, offering the best evidence to date about which genes play a significant role in the condition.. The research, funded by multiple governments and nonprofit foundations, involved a collaboration among hundreds of scientists and included pooling genetic data from nearly 37,000 people with schizophrenia.
