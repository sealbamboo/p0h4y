Should top execs be reimbursed for taxes triggered by tax inversion deals? The payments, or gross ups, would cover a 15% tax imposed on stock and option awards to officials at companies pursuing inversions.. Medtronic will cover a $24.8 billion tax bill for Omar Ishrak, its chief executive, and other executives will split another $32.7 million, while 10 non-employee board members will share about $5.5 million. And AbbVie disclosed in a recent filing that similar tax bills will be covered for Richard Gonzalez, the chief executive, and other executives and directors. But should these reimbursements be made?
