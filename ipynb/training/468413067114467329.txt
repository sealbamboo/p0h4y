A push to widen access to experimental drugs for people with terminal illness is gaining traction among lawmakers in several states highlighting a growing clamor from patients for promising therapies that havent been approved for sale The socalled righttotry bills the first of which was signed into law in Colorado on Saturday by Gov John W Hickenlooper may not make a big difference in practice because they generally dont require companies to provide access to the unapproved drugs outside of clinical trials
