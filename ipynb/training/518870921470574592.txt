 Becton, Dickinson & Co. agreed to buy CareFusion Corp. in a $12.2 billion deal designed to provide a full range of medical products to hospitals, which are under pressure to cut costs and improve quality.. Becton, based in Franklin Lakes, N.J., will pay $58 in cash and stock for each share of San Diego-based CareFusion, based on Becton’s closing share price Friday. CareFusion shares rose 1.8% to $46.17 in New York Stock Exchange trading Friday. Becton shares finished up 1.5% at $115.84.
