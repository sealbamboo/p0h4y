Massachusetts, the first state to have an Obamacare-like health system, is also among the states struggling the most to implement the federal health law.. The state's website is such a morass that Massachusetts officials have so far only had to pay a fraction of the bill to contractor CGI Federal, a unit of CGI Group Inc., which to date has received just $11 million of a $69 million contract. Only 2,800 people have enrolled so far on the state's new health exchange
