Robert Sigel talks with Dr. Alicia Quesnel of the <a href="http://www.masseyeandear.org/for-patients/patient-guide/patient-education/diseases-and-conditions/noise_trauma/" target="_blank">Massachusetts Eye and Ear Infirmary</a>, about the patients she's treating for hearing loss from the Boston Marathon explosions. They discuss the scope of these injuries and the mechanics of hearing loss generally.
