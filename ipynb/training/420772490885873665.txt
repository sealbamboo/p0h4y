 WellPoint Inc. will sell contact-lens and eyewear retailer 1-800 Contacts Inc. to private-equity firm Thomas H. Lee Partners, unloading a company it bought for around $900 million less than two years ago.. The Indianapolis, Ind., insurer also agreed to sell the glasses.com website and its technology for helping people try on glasses virtually, which were part of the 1-800 Contacts purchase, to Italian eyewear company Luxottica Group SpA.
