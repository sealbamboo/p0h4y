 Intuitive Surgical Inc. said it received U.S. Food and Drug Administration clearance for a new model of its da Vinci surgical robot that allows wider use of the device and addresses previous performance issues. . The da Vinci Xi System is the fourth version of Intuitive's surgical robot, which is operated by surgeons from a computer console to perform minimally invasive procedures like prostate surgery.
