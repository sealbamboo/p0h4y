The head of the Department of Veterans Affairs asked Congress Wednesday for thousands more employees and billions of dollars in extra funding to fix systemic problems at his embattled organization.. At a Senate Committee on Veterans's Affairs hearing, Sloan Gibson, acting VA Secretary, said the department needs an additional $17.6 billion over the next three years which will pay for 10,000 clinical staff members to properly address problems highlighted over the past few months by investigations, internal reviews and complaints...
