The patient comes in for a checkup on the lungs and a scan reveals a clogged artery A test for a teens concussion finds a brain tumor An Xray of an ER patients broken ribs shows a mass on the kidneys  They are all known as incidental findings because they are abnormalities discovered unintentionally and not related to the medical condition that prompted the test They occur in as many as a third of imaging tests studies show But they often arent followed up on or even noted in a patients record
