TORONTO—Tim Hortons Inc. Tuesday outlined a broad-based growth plan that includes adding at least 800 stores globally over the next five years, developing more nutritious menu offerings and boosting its position in the key U.S. market.. The Canadian coffee and doughnut chain said it would adapt to changes in consumer tastes and demographics with new, healthier menu items to spur growth in its core home market. It also reiterated that it is committed to the U.S. market, the largest quick-service restaurant market in the world...
