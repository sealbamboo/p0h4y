WASHINGTONThe US mission in Liberia to combat Ebola could end as early as next summer the top American commander in Africa said Wednesday signaling the confidence US officials have that the global effort under way will contain the virus Army Gen David Rodriguez head of the US Africa Command said the US has scaled back its original mission in Liberia Most of the engineering and logistics projects will be completed by January he told reporters at the Pentagon and some service members will begin to return home
