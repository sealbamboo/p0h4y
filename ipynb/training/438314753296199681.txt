We all know we should eat healthier exercise more and sometimes take medicines to stave off heart disease Some researchers think putting an age on our hearts will do a better job of motivating us to do all that In a recent study involving  patients researchers in Spain compared a relatively new tool to measure heartdisease risk called Heart Age against a commonly used riskassessment tool Participants who were told their heart age was greater than their chronological age showed bigger health improvements a year
