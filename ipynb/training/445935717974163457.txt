Spring blooms mixed with lingering winter germs create the perfect scene for pinkeye Itchy swollen runny eyes are technically known as conjunctivitis which can actually take three forms viral bacterial and allergic The allergic variety which isnt contagious often surfaces when flowers start to reemerge The other forms are highly contagious Richard Abbott clinical professor of ophthalmology at the University of California San Francisco and past president of the American Academy of Ophthalmology says this season so far hasnt been markedly worse than others but it isnt over yet Here is one experts opinion
