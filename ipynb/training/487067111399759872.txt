Severe obesity among New York City public school students in kindergarten through eighth grade declined in the five years ended in 2011, according to a new peer-reviewed study being released Thursday.. The rate of severe obesity dipped to 5.7% of these children, from 6.3%. Obesity also fell, to 20.7% from 21.9%. These problems were most acute among minorities, boys and poor children, said the study, in the journal Preventing Chronic Disease.
