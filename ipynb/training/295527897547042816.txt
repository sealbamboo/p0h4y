Heres the deal Your overall goal is to burn as many calories during a workout as possible The best way to do this is to exercise as hard and as long as you can and for this you need readily available energy Stored body fat certainly is a source of energy but its not the source your body turns to first when its aiming for speed and endurance For that it needs carbs Unlike fat stores which are in endless supply unless youre emaciated carb stores are limited and need to be replenished This is especially important if you work out in the morning when your carb stores are at their lowest My recommendation Eat a small carbrich snack like a piece of fruit before a workout This will power you through a typical workout without loading you up with too many extra calories  Learn more in the Everyday Health
