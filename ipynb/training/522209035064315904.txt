The U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention is intensifying its Ebola response to include dispatching an infection-control team to any U.S. hospital with a confirmed case, and conceded that such a measure might have prevented a Dallas nurse from contracting the virus.. CDC Director Tom Frieden said Tuesday the team would monitor and train on-the-ground health workers, a precaution he wished had been taken at Texas Health Presbyterian Hospital Dallas. “We will put a team on the ground within hours,” Dr. Frieden said....
