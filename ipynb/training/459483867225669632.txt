The Food and Drug Administration on Thursday approved the first DNA test for human papillomavirus or HPV that could be used instead of the Pap test in a cervicalcancer screening program But the test isnt widely expected to take the place of the Papthe standard screening for cervical canceranytime soon That is partly because the leading physicians group on the topic the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists recommends that the new HPV screening be used along with the Pap test
