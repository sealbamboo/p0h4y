At least 7.5 million people have signed up for health insurance through federal and state health-care exchanges, Health and Human Services Secretary Kathleen Sebelius said Thursday.. Mrs. Sebelius provided the new figures in testimony before the Senate Finance Committee about the agency's 2015 budget request.
