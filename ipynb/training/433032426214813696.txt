Amid the news last summer of Qatars support for Islamist Arab uprisings its change of leadership and the US expansion of TV network Al Jazeera a small research discovery in the Persian Gulf state got lost among the hubbub In June researchers at the Qatar Biomedical Research Institute or QBRI instructed stem cells in a laboratory setting to secrete insulin in order to control bloodsugar levelsa process usually performed by the pancreas in healthy individuals but lacking among diabetics
