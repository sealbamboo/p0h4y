Federal prosecutors have charged two Turkish citizens with distributing illegal prescription drugs in the U.S., including illegal cancer medications smuggled from Turkey and other countries.. The men were charged with importing foreign-made cancer drugs that are not approved for sale by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration, because they aren't made in FDA-inspected facilities or packaged in FDA-approved packaging.
