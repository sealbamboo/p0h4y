The first person diagnosed in the U.S. with the deadly Middle East respiratory syndrome, or MERS, is improving and expected to be released from an Indiana hospital soon, health officials reported Monday.. The man is a health-care worker who lives in Saudi Arabia, the epicenter of the newly emergent virus, and works at a hospital in Riyadh.
