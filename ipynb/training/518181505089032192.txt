Arkansas lawyer Brian Reddick has found a lucrative niche as America ages: suing big nursing-home chains.. Mr. Reddick and other alumni of a Florida law firm that pioneered the approach have taken those tactics on the road. They are filing neglect and abuse cases in places like Pennsylvania, where the country’s fourth-largest concentration of residents aged 85 and older has spurred a litigation boom aimed largely at for-profit nursing-home operators.
