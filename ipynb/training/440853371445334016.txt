Wearable devices have always been a niche accessory, appealing to the techy and fitness-conscious set. Now as the sector takes off, consumers can expect a wave of even more specific fitness trackers as new competitors try to grab a piece of the market.. Unlike more general fitness trackers like the Nike FuelBand or Fitbit, quite a few of this year’s devices tend to target an ultra-specific type of user, such as a cycler, weight lifter or someone who slouches..
