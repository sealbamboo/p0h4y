A Food and Drug Administration advisory committee voted Tuesday that there isnt enough evidence to say the antiinflammatory painkiller naproxen is safer than other pain drugs when it comes to heart health The committee vote was  that naproxen sold as Aleve by Bayer AG cant be convincingly proven to have greater cardiac safety than ibuprofen Celebrex and other nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drugs Ibuprofen is sold under brand names including Advil and Motrin
