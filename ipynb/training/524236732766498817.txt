Last month CVS Health purged its  pharmacies of tobacco products in a gambit to embrace a commitment to improving public health and generate goodwill But the move comes with a price  an estimated  billion in annual sales will be sacrificed Now the company is hoping to find a way to compensate for that bet Its Caremark pharmacy benefits manager which is one of the largest in the US will soon require some customers to make a  copayment on any prescription that is filled at a pharmacy selling cigarettes and other tobacco products
