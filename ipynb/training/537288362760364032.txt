Thanksgiving stuffing is a favorite tradition but if its stuffed in the bird or made with meatstock its off limits to vegetarian and vegan diners  Amber St Peter of Long Beach Calif offers this fresh take on a traditional holiday stuffing thats suitable for everyone at the table including those who want to eat glutenfree It uses quinoa instead of bread while butternut squash celeriac and hazelnuts capture the flavors of fall Ms St Peter a vegan made the dish for her family and even the meateaters loved it This dish actually impressed them she says Its hearty without the bread or gluten and flavorful without a bunch of processed ingredients It was a crowd pleaser the first year and will be a permanent fixture at our table for years to come
