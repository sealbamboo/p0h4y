
 This week the Recipes for Health columnist Martha Rose Shulman turns to a summer weekend staple the grill to make pizzas She writes Here are five ways to make pizza without going near an oven  A basic pizza recipe you can use to create whatever flavor combinations you like  No precooked sauce is needed for these pies  The grilled eggplant slices are equally good on their own or use them instead of crusts for eggplant pizzas  Cooking the onions on the grill before assembling the pizza give them a nice charred flavor  Imported black olives are optional but they add a briny punch that complements the sweet fennel
