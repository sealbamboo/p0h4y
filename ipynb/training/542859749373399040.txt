A Department of Veterans Affairs hospital project near Denver came to a standstill this week after federal judges ruled the VA was in breach of contract, hundreds of millions of dollars over budget and devoid of a plan to adequately fund the facility.. The billion-dollar project is one of four large VA construction plans that in recent years have faced cost and schedule overruns, as well as scrutiny by legislators and government auditors.
