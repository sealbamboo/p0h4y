With her husband often away on long trips and her daughter off to college Sue Halpern knew she needed to find something else to do with her life something that would help banish the empty nest syndrome she and her dog Pransky were feeling acutely  My dog needed a job Halpern said We both needed something new to do That something turned out to be a boon not only for Halpern and her dog but also the older folks they help as they travel from nursing home to nursing home  Halpern has written about Pranksys transformation into a therapy dog in her new book A Dog Walks Into a Nursing Home Lessons in the Good Life from an Unlikely Teacher NBCs Jane Pauley reports on their progress
