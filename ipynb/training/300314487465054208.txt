Researchers analyzed 12 years of data collected from nearly 500 women, ages 44 to 57, in Britain and found that 63 percent of those 50 and older reported persistent, incident or intermittent knee pain.. Forty-four percent of the women said they had experienced "any pain" and 23 percent said they had knee pain on most days of the previous month.
