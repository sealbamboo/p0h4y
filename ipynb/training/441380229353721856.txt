CVS, the nation's second-largest pharmacy chain, said Wednesday it would stop selling all cigarettes and tobacco products nationwide by October, saying they have no place in a drugstore company that is trying to become more of a health-care provider.. The move is a bold and expensive one for CVS, a unit of CVS Caremark Corp.
