Scientists hoping to eliminate dengue fever, a debilitating disease of the tropics that has made its way to parts of the U.S., are launching an extensive eradication experiment in different regions of the world.. The trial comes as a deadly outbreak of dengue fever is hitting Malaysia and as cases recently reached Japan, the country's first outbreak in 70 years. The mosquito-borne disease also has appeared in some southern U.S. states, including Florida and Texas.
