The parents of a fivemonthold boy who died from acute rickets have admitted his manslaughter Nkosiyapha Kunene  of Erith southeast London appeared at the Old Bailey accused of killing Ndingeko Kunene on  June  His wife Virginia Kunene  admitted the same offence at an earlier hearing  The Zimbabweborn couple will be sentenced on  February The judge Mr Justice Singh told them he was considering all options The seriousness of the offence of which the life of a baby is lost will be apparent to everyone so I must bear that in mind Both defendants were granted conditional bail Rickets is a bone condition caused by a deficiency of vitamin D and can be brought on by a poor diet
