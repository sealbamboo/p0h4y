A Mississippi child born with the AIDS virus who scientists thought might have been cured following drug treatment has shown signs of infection again in a disappointing turn for researchers who had hoped the case would identify one path to a cure  In March doctors said the young girl was still free of the virus even though she hadnt been on any antiretroviral drugs for about two years But doctors found detectable levels of HIV the virus that causes AIDS in her blood during a routine checkup earlier this month
