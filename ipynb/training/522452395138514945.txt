The second Texas health-care worker to be diagnosed with Ebola flew from Dallas to Cleveland and back in the days before she reported symptoms, the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention said on Wednesday.. The woman flew on Frontier Airlines flight 1142 from Dallas-Fort Worth airport to Cleveland on Oct. 10, the CDC said.
